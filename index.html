The Land of SkyIslands

Welcome to the world where mountains, gardens and castles mysteriously float in the air. It is a place of many wonders for you to explore. Some of the magical planes are accessible by browser. However, in order to enter fully this world, you will need a magical Google Cardboard viewing device.

In the future, some realms of SkyIslands will also be reachable with an enchanted Oculus Rift or a GearVR of all-seeing.

SkyIslandsVR is an ongoing project by artist and designer Erica Layton. The project is comprised of multiple parts on multiple VR platforms, and is still expanding.

Check out the webVR scenes below, built with a-frame.io

Islands Over Water

This scene can be viewed in the browser or with Google Cardboard. In the browser, you can fly around in the scene using the ancient and powerful W A S D keys. Click and drag to look around.
