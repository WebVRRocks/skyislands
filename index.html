The Lands of SkyIslands

Welcome to the world where mountains, gardens and castles mysteriously float in the air. It is a place of many wonders for you to explore. Some of the magical planes are accessible by a Web browser.

In order to fully enter the realms of WebVR, you will need a magical Google Cardboard-viewing device or the enchanted higher-end devices such as the Oculus Rift, HTC Vive, Samsung Gear Gear VR, or Google Daydream of all-seeing.

SkyIslandsVR is an ongoing project by artist and designer, Erica Layton. The project is comprised of multiple parts on multiple VR platforms, and is still expanding.

Check out the WebVR scenes below, built with A-Frame.

Islands Over Water

This scene can be viewed in the browser or with Google Cardboard. In a desktop browser, you can fly around in the scene using the ancient and powerful arrow keys (and click and drag to look around).
